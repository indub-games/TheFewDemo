## About This Game

**The Few** is a World War II combined-arms combat game. Take the controls of authentic single and multi-engine aircraft, man the anti-aircraft guns, warships, tanks, and fight across every major theater of the war — from the Battle of Britain to North Africa, the Mediterranean, the Eastern Front, and the Pacific.

This is a deliberate alternative to free-to-play vehicular combat. There are no premium vehicles. There is no grind to unlock the Spitfire. There are no battle passes, no boosters, no consumables. You pay once, and you have the whole game.

### Pillars

**Authentic Flight**
A custom flight model with per-aircraft aerodynamics, engine spool, prop-synchronized gun timing, and a six-stage damage state machine. Take a wing-root hit and watch your aileron response slacken. Take an oil line and watch the temperature climb until the engine seizes. Pull negative G in a Spitfire and watch your guns cough. The simulation is honest without being punishing — accessible on a gamepad, deeper on stick-and-throttle.

**Combined Arms**
Fighters are not the only role. Fly Heinkel He 111s and Junkers Ju 87 Stukas in attack runs against Royal Navy convoys. Crew the bow and stern AA emplacements of a destroyer and try to keep the Stukas off your ship. The same mission can be played from the cockpit, the bombsight, or the gun mount.

**Multi-Front WW2**
Battle of Britain at launch — Spitfires, Hurricanes, Bf 109s, He 111s, Stukas, Royal Navy destroyers. Theaters added post-launch are free for owners: North Africa, the Mediterranean, the Eastern Front, the Pacific. Iconic aircraft and ships of every major combatant, no premium tier.

**Honest Pricing**
- Single premium purchase
- Full vehicle roster, full campaign, full multiplayer
- Cosmetic skins only — never gameplay-affecting DLC
- No subscription, no currency, no ads, no boosters

### Modes

- **Campaign** — scripted missions across each theater, playable solo or co-op
- **Duel** — head-to-head ranked dogfighting
- **Combined Arms PvP** — mixed air/sea matches against other players
- **Tutorial** — full flight school for players new to the genre

### Built In Unreal Engine 5

Modern lighting, custom physics, dedicated server multiplayer, gamepad and HOTAS support out of the box.
